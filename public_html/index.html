GAME GUIDE

The Last of Us (2013) is a third-person action-adventure survival horror video game designed exclusively for PlayStationTM. It is the game of the decade (2010-2020), developed by Naughty Dog.
Currently, the game has been rebuilt for PS5TM and will launch in October along with a live-action adaptation by HBO MAX.
